What is Tornado Music?

Tornado Music is a digital music service that gives you access to tons of songs and other content from creators all over the world. Basic functions such as playing music are totally free. Tornado Music premier's Trending Songs Globally. Our Music Library allows you to enjoy music you love without any Interruptions like Ads. You can also listen to the Playlists of your Favorite Artists and You will experience Calming Interface with our Aesthetic Tornado Music Player Which also provides Lyrics for Every Song out there.
